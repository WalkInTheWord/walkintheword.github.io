Walk in the Word

Read. Listen. Understand. Daily.

Experience the Bible like never before with Walk in the Word — a mobile app that lets you read, listen, and study scripture with ease. Whether you’re on your morning walk or deep in study, our synced audio + reading experience helps bring the Word to life.

Download for iOS Download for Android

Key Features

  • Word-for-word translations: ESV, NASB, NKJV, KJV
  • Audio Bible playback with 2x speed control
  • Reading plans: 30-day, 6-month, 1-year, chronological
  • Strong’s Concordance: Greek and Hebrew roots
  • Notes, highlights, and verse sharing
  • No ads, no tracking, offline-friendly

Why Walk in the Word?

We believe everyone should have access to Scripture — clearly, securely, and without distractions. Walk in the Word supports your daily devotion, in-depth study, and spiritual growth journey.
